About Our Practice

Dr. Taylor holds a Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery with over 12 years of experience in family medicine. We believe in a patient-first approach, ensuring that every individual receives the time and attention they deserve.

Medical Aid & Insurance

We are contracted with major providers including Discovery, Bonitas, Momentum, and Medihelp. Private patients are welcome and can enquire about rates at our reception.

How to use the Patient Portal

To streamline your visit, we use a digital portal for new patients:

  1. Book: Choose your slot on the Booking page.
  2. Redirect: If you're new, you'll be sent to the Portal.
  3. Details: Fill in your history and insurance once.
  4. Done: Your doctor receives a secure PDF immediately.
